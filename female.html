Injury Prevention and Safety

When performing workouts, especially with high intensity or heavy weights, it is important to focus on injury prevention and proper form:

  • Always warm up before starting your workout to prepare your muscles and reduce the risk of injury.
  • Use proper form and technique for each exercise to avoid strain and injury. Consider working with a trainer if you are unsure.
  • Listen to your body and avoid pushing through pain. Rest and recovery are crucial to prevent overuse injuries.
  • Gradually increase the intensity of your workouts to allow your body to adapt and prevent sudden injuries.
