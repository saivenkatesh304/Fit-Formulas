Triceps Workouts

Long Head Workouts

Overhead Triceps Extension

This exercise targets the long head of the triceps and helps in developing the upper part of the triceps.

Movement Category: Primary

Programming: 3-4 sets of 10-12 repetitions

Weight: Choose a weight that allows full extension without compromising form.

To do overhead triceps extensions:

  • Stand with your feet shoulder-width apart and hold a dumbbell with both hands above your head.
  • Lower the dumbbell behind your head by bending your elbows.
  • Extend your arms back to the starting position.

Skull Crushers

Skull crushers are effective for isolating the long head of the triceps, improving strength and size.

Movement Category: Primary

Programming: 3-4 sets of 8-12 repetitions

Weight: Use a weight that challenges you while maintaining good form.

To do skull crushers:

  • Lie on a bench and hold a barbell with a close grip above your chest.
  • Lower the barbell towards your forehead by bending your elbows.
  • Extend your arms back to the starting position.

Lateral Head Workouts

Triceps Pushdowns

Triceps pushdowns are excellent for targeting the lateral head of the triceps and improving overall muscle definition.

Movement Category: Primary

Programming: 3-4 sets of 10-15 repetitions

Weight: Use a weight that allows full extension without compromising form.

To do triceps pushdowns:

  • Stand facing a cable machine with a high pulley.
  • Grab the handle with an overhand grip and push it down towards your thighs.
  • Slowly return the handle to the starting position.

Bench Dips

Bench dips target the lateral head and provide a great bodyweight exercise for triceps strength.

Movement Category: Primary

Programming: 3-4 sets of 8-12 repetitions

Weight: Bodyweight; add extra weight if needed using a weight belt.

To do bench dips:

  • Position your hands shoulder-width apart on a bench behind you.
  • Extend your legs out in front and lower your body by bending your elbows.
  • Push back up to the starting position.

Medial Head Workouts

Close-Grip Bench Press

The close-grip bench press emphasizes the medial head of the triceps and can help build overall triceps mass.

Movement Category: Primary

Programming: 3-4 sets of 8-12 repetitions

Weight: Use a weight that allows you to maintain good form throughout the set.

To do close-grip bench press:

  • Lie on a bench and hold a barbell with a close grip.
  • Lower the barbell to your chest and push it back up.

Diamond Push-Ups

Diamond push-ups are an effective bodyweight exercise for isolating the medial head of the triceps.

Movement Category: Primary

Programming: 3-4 sets of 10-15 repetitions

Weight: Bodyweight; add weight if needed using a weight vest.

To do diamond push-ups:

  • Get into a push-up position with your hands close together under your chest.
  • Lower your body towards the floor and push back up.

Overall Triceps Workouts

Triceps Kickbacks

Triceps kickbacks are great for overall triceps development, targeting all three heads of the triceps.

Movement Category: Primary

Programming: 3-4 sets of 10-15 repetitions

Weight: Use a weight that allows for controlled movements.

To do triceps kickbacks:

  • Stand with one knee and hand on a bench and hold a dumbbell in the other hand.
  • Extend your arm behind you while keeping your elbow stationary.
  • Slowly return to the starting position.

Triceps Dips

Triceps dips target all three heads of the triceps and are excellent for overall muscle development.

Movement Category: Primary

Programming: 3-4 sets of 8-12 repetitions

Weight: Bodyweight; add additional weight if needed using a weight belt.

To do triceps dips:

  • Use parallel bars and lift yourself up with your arms extended.
  • Lower your body until your elbows are at 90 degrees.
  • Push yourself back up to the starting position.
